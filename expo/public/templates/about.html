Our Story

Founded in 2018 with the vision of bridging geographical gaps in the exhibition industry, ExpoSphere has evolved from a small tech startup into a global leader in trade connectivity.

We recognized early on that traditional exhibitions were becoming inaccessible due to rising travel costs and environmental concerns. Our platform was built to solve these challenges, ensuring that face-to-face connection—the heart of business—can happen anywhere, anytime.

Today, we support thousands of organizations in showcasing their innovations to a global audience, proving that physical distance is no longer a barrier to progress.
